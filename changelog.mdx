<Update label="March 2026" description="v2.12.0">
  ## Updates Collection Manager

  - The counters for "Haves", "Wants", "Total", and "Price Shift" automatically update based on the selected filters.
  - Active filters in the collection are now synchronized with the filter when adding cards.
  - In selection mode, the total is now displayed, and the selected cards remain selected even when switching to another page in the collection.
  - The "Select All" checkbox now always allows you to deselect all cards on the current page (minus symbol). Previously, this was only possible if all cards were selected.
  - Rarity filter: Multiple selections can now be combined using an "OR" operator
  - Rarity filter: Counts for Holo and Non-Holo have been added
  - For the condition filter, you can now also select "less than or equal to" or "greater than or equal to".
  - New filter: Type (Monster, Spell, Trap, Ability)
  - Card Type filter: Multiple selections are now treated as an “OR” operator.
  - Card Type Filter: Main Deck and Extra Deck added
  - New Filter: Artwork Category (All, Alternative Artwork, Extended Art, Lost Art, Original)
  - To improve readability, the print number is now displayed to the left of the card name when adding cards.
</Update>

<Update label="January 2026" description="v2.10.0">
  ## Ads

  We understand that nobody likes advertising. That's why cardcluster has been ad-free for eight years (!). We still have great plans for cardcluster and want to use this additional form of monetization to achieve these plans more quickly (e.g., expanding to other TCGs).

  Starting this year, we are working with an advertising provider that specializes in the gaming sector. This means that, in addition to classic banner ads, you will also regularly see interesting gaming campaigns (e.g., when new games are released).

  We are introducing advertising gradually and want to communicate this transparently. Currently, only guests see ads. This means you can **simply sign up** and see **no ads**. Just like before! Just so you know: We may show ads to registered users later on. Subscribers will of course not see ads under any circumstances. Not even in the future.

  Advertising allows us to offer you more features for free, and we will gradually expand this. We are happy to announce that the gallery view for decks is now available to everyone for free!

  As always, if you have any questions, we are here for you via [email](https://cardcluster.com/support) or on our Discord server.
</Update>

<Update label="June 2025" description="v2.7.0">
  ### Translations for all languages

  Translations for French, Italian, Spanish, and Portuguese are now live. Please give us feedback if you notice any errors or inaccuracies in the translations.

  ### Local deck communities

  Our goal is for everyone to be able to post decks and comments in their own language. That's why you now have two options when creating a deck:

  1. Post a deck in **your language**: The deck will be visible within your community. The community is based on the language you're using.
  2. Post a deck in **English**: The deck will be visible everywhere on cardcluster. This way, you can reach the largest possible audience.

  The language of the deck is automatically detected.

  For public decks, you will see decks in your own language and English by default (setting: `My Community`). If you change the new setting to `All Decks`, you will see all decks just as before.

  Please note that this feature is primarily relevant to non-English-speaking communities on cardcluster.

  ### Feedback appreciated section

  If you would like to have feedback on your deck from the community, there is now a new section under Top Decks. Simply write a short comment under your deck explaining what you need help with. The comment will also be highlighted under [Community](https://cardcluster.com/community).

  ### Retro Formats

  - The banlist status of Edison and Goat is now displayed on the card page. These decks are also highlighted (similar to Master Duel decks) and can appear on the front page.
  - Under Top Decks, you will find a new section for retro formats. We have added 5 formats, but we will be adding more next week.
</Update>

<Update label="May 2025" description="v2.6.0">
  ## Additional price data

  In addition to the existing prices from Cardmarket and TCGplayer, more data is now available. This gives you an even better idea of a card's actual value!

  **Cardmarket:** Default, AVG, Low, Trend, AVG1, AVG7 and AVG30.

  **TCGplayer:** Default, Low, Mid, High, Market and Direct Low.

  You can find more detailed explanations about card prices on the help page: [Card Prices](https://cardcluster.com/en/collection-manager/card-prices).

  ### New features

  - You can select the price data separately for the entire page and for each collection.
  - Hover or click on a price to view other price data.
  - On the card page graph, you can select multiple price data points at the same time to compare their history. To do this, hover or click on the price next to the graph and select the data points you are interested in.
  - The selected time period is now automatically saved and will not reset.
  - New period: 2 years
  - In the header, you can now see how many cards you have collected and quickly access your current wants list via the heart icon.
</Update>
