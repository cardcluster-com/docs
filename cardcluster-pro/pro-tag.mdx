---
title: Change Pro Tag design
---

As a cardcluster Pro member the Pro Tag will be displayed behind your username. Every 6 months you will unlock a new design that you can select under [Edit profile](https://cardcluster.com/settings/profile). We only count months during which your subscription was active without interruption. Once you have unlocked a design, you will keep it and can select it even after pausing your subscription.
