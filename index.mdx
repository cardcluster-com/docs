If you haven't already done so, the first thing you should do is [create an account](https://cardcluster.com/signup). It takes less than 30 seconds, is free, and gives you many immediate benefits:

- Use cardcluster without ads
- Create and post your own decks
- Save interesting decks to your favorites or create your own versions
- Interact with our huge community
