---
title: Community Guidelines
---


Thank you for being part of the cardcluster community! We want to create a pleasant atmosphere where everyone can have fun building decks and sharing ideas.

Please always be kind and respectful and follow the guidelines below and our [Terms of Use](https://cardcluster.com/terms), when posting comments or creating a deck guide on cardcluster.

* The content of your comment must relate to the deck and be relevant.
* No politics or religion.
* The comment section is not a chat. Spam or low quality comments are not allowed.
* Use links sparingly and only when absolutely necessary.
* Do not criticize without a suggestion for improvement.
* Everyone is allowed to play any kind of deck. Please respect that.
* Some users may not want help with deck building. Please respect that.
* Always pay attention to what type of deck it is. For example, some decks are labeled as casual decks and deliberately do not want to play the best cards.

<Info>
    You contribute to our community by voting up good comments, voting down low quality comments and reporting comments that violate our guidelines.
</Info>

Some comments must be approved by a moderator before they appear publicly. This decision is made automatically by our system when the comment is created. Please note: Just because your comment needs to be approved does not necessarily mean that you have violated a rule.

Users who violate these rules must expect that their comments will more frequently require manual approval. In the case of severe violations, users may be suspended from the comments for a certain period of time or completely.
