On cardcluster you will find a [large deck building community](https://cardcluster.com/decks) where you can share your decks,
exchange ideas and discover new creative decks. Here are some tips if you want to post a deck.

## Visibility

If you create a deck, it will be **public** by default. To make your deck *private* or *not listed*, you need
a [cardcluster Pro membership](https://cardcluster.com/pricing).

Every day, many decks are created on cardcluster. To prevent our public deck library from being flooded with unfinished decks,
your deck must meet a few quality criteria before it is visible:

- Use a **suitable deck name**. Not suitable names are e.g. „Unnamed Deck“ or „Cyber Dragon Copy“
- The deck must be **legal**. You can check the status in the Deck Builder.
- The Main Deck must contain 40 or more cards.
- The Extra Deck must contain exactly 15 cards. Exceptions: Retro formats (e.g. GOAT) or when you play `Domain of True Monarchs`, `The Monarchs Erupt`, `Re-Cover` or `Anchamoufrite` your Extra Deck can be empty.
- Your deck must not be an exact copy of another deck without you having made any changes.
- You can publish one deck per day without [cardcluster Pro](https://cardcluster.com/pricing).

<Info>
	Even if your deck does not meet the quality criteria, it will still be public and can be seen on your profile from other users.
</Info>

## When will my deck be displayed on the front page?

The front page shows TCG and Master Duel decks that use the current ban list, as well as Goat and Edison decks. The "Trending decks" section
will show the Top 3 decks with the most likes within the last 2 weeks (regardless of the banlist).

## Tips for more views, comments and likes

- Write a **guide or a short description** for your deck. Decks with guides are especially highlighted and are much more interesting
than a simple deck list without context.
- Build high-quality, well thought-out decks. Quality over quantity.
- Exchange with other users in the comment section and share your knowledge!
- Become a [Pro member](https://cardcluster.com/pricing). Decks with cover card, avatar and Pro tag are highly visible.
- Report from your last tournament or locals. Tell us how your match-ups went and what experiences you had with your deck.
- Ask the community for advice with deck building! We have many helpful users who are happy to give you tips.
